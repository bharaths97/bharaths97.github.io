About Me

Welcome! I'm Bharath Suresh, a recent Cybersecurity graduate from Rochester Institute of Technology with background and experience developing enterprise-focussed cybersecurity solutions. Passionate about safeguarding the digital landscape, I bring a wealth of experience and knowledge from both academic pursuits and professional engagements.

During my time as a graduate student, I have had the opportunity to delve into researching unexplored intersections of different fields with cybersecurity like integrating elements of Sanskrit language into cryptographic algorithms, and cryptic crossword puzzles as a covert communication channels. Apart from the exciting projects I have done, the courses like Cryptography, Computer Forensics and Computer System Security that I undertook have given me a broad understanding of the different facets of cybersecurity.

In my professional journey, I've worked iSecure LLC, Rochester Institute of Technology, Chegg Inc., and Zoho Corporation's ManageEngine, where I've assissted customers fortify their cyber infrastructures, performed peneteration testing, implemented real-time network monitoring solutions, implemented cryptographic solutions for EV communication and led the development of enterprise security software products.
